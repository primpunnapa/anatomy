Cranial Nerves Matching Game

How to Play

Match the cranial nerve names to their correct positions on the anatomy diagram.

Steps:

  1. Drag a cranial nerve name from the options on the right.
  2. Drop it onto the correct labeled position on the diagram.
  3. If the match is correct, the label will update with the nerve name. If not, try again!

Match all the cranial nerves to complete the game. Good luck!
