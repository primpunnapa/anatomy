Foramina/Canal Matching Game

How to Play

Match the foramina/canal to the correct cranial nerve on the anatomy diagram.

Steps:

  1. Drag a foramina/canal and cranial nerve from the options on the right.
  2. Drop it onto the correct labeled position on the diagram.
  3. If the match is correct, the label will update with the foramina/canal and cranial nerve. If not, try again!

Match all the foramina/canal and cranial nerves to complete the game. Good luck!
